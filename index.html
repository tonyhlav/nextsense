About us

The Aquila ITC difference begins and ends with its Consultants

Aquila believes firmly that an innovative creative mind is the primary requirement to an excellent consultant. No degree, certification, or training class can provide the initiative and desire necessary to stay on top of the Security game. We do value training and believe it is a great way to get people interested in the field, however we know that the level of competence we expect can only be derived from a deep inner interest in all aspects of Security. From covert exfiltration and exploitation to countering those threats, both sides require creative thought and a deep understanding of the attacker mindset.

Conflict evolves along similar parallels regardless if it is in physical space or over the Internet. Most organizations are painfully unaware of their attack surface, and further are unaware of the capabilities that they can currently apply to combat these threats. This leads to the obvious conflict issue of being unaware of the attack, and unaware of how to defend against it. Security must be internalized into the organization and the risks must be effectively communicated to the proper stakeholders.
